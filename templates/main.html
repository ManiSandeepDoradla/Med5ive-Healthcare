Lab Tests

Accurate diagnostics with fast turnaround and certified reporting

🩸 Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Evaluates overall health and detects a range of disorders, including anemia and infections.

🧪 Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)

Checks glucose, electrolytes, kidney and liver function to assess metabolic health.

🧬 Thyroid Profile (TSH, T3, T4)

Measures thyroid hormones to diagnose hypo/hyperthyroidism and related conditions.

💉 Lipid Profile

Assesses cholesterol and triglycerides to evaluate cardiovascular risk.

🧫 HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin)

Shows average blood glucose over 2–3 months for diabetes monitoring.

🧴 Vitamin D & B12

Detects deficiencies impacting bone health, energy levels, and neurological function.
